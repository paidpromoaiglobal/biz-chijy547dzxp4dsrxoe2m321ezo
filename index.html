Our Story

Swastik Diamond

Swastik Diamond is a premier diamond manufacturer and jewellery establishment located in the heart of Surat's renowned diamond district — Mahidharpura. With a massive collection of lab-grown diamonds, including CVD and HPHT varieties, Swastik Diamond has built a solid reputation for delivering flawlessly cut, polished, and certified diamonds to businesses and individual buyers alike. Our commitment to quality, variety, and transparency has earned us a perfect 5-star rating from our valued customers.

Situated at Blue Diamond, Hathfaliya, Opposite Krishna Cinema in Surat, we serve diamond traders, jewellers, and retailers across India who demand the best in lab-grown diamonds. Whether you are looking for investment-grade stones or diamonds to elevate your jewellery business, Swastik Diamond is your one-stop destination. We take pride in offering budget-friendly pricing without ever compromising on the quality that our customers have come to trust and rely upon.
